Our Second Ride - Burnhamthorpe Multi-Use Trail

Date Thu 02 January 2020 Tags Ride

Register for the Event

Our second ride will depart from Mississauga Valley Community Centre on Jan 12 2020 at 1:30PM. The majority of the route will be along the Burnhamthorpe multi-use trail with some exploration of surrounding neighbourhoods. Our midway destination will be Wow Wow Yum Yum as voted by our participants.

There are bike racks available to lock up your ride at the main entrance to Mississauga Valley CC. We'll meetup within the lobby just inside the main entrance and head out for our ride at 1:30 sharp.
