Strava

Please consider joining our Strava Club. You can use Strava to track your progress, motivate you, and connect with others. When using Strava you can use their app to track your GPS position and collect all sorts of stats. There is also an option to track your km's manually if you don't have a phone or are privacy concious.

Please be aware of the two privacy features that Strava offers too. The first allows you to approve who can follow you before they can see your profile, whilst the second gives you the ability to set a privacy area around your home. Anyone viewing your activity will then only see where you went outside of this zone. Both of these options can be accessed by visiting the Privacy tab under Settings.
