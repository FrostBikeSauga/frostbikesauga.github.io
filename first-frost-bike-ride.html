Our First Ride - Queensway Multi-Use Trail

Date Sun 01 December 2019 Tags Ride

Our inaugural ride will depart from Huron Park Community Centre on Dec 15 2019 at 1:30PM. The majority of the route will be along the Queensway multi-use trail with some exploration of surrounding neighbourhoods. Our midway destination will be La Casa Dolce or Daddy O Doughnuts depending on feedback received from our participants.

There are bike racks available to lock up your ride at the main entrance to Huron Park CC. We'll meetup within the lobby just inside the main entrance and head out for our ride at 1:30 sharp.
