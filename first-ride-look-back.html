Successful First Ride

Date Fri 13 December 2019 Tags Ride

Thank you to all of our participants that attended our very first ride on Dec 8th. We had 9 riders participate in a leisurely ride from Huron Park to La Casa Dolce and back. Weather for the ride was a balmy temperature of approximately 5°C and the pathways were nice and clear for our travels. Amongst the riders we had attend we had many riders from local Mississauga community rides and slow rolls participate.

Our next ride will take place Jan 12 at 1:30PM departing from Mississauga Valley Community Centre. We will be riding west along the Burnhamthorpe multi-use trail with a midway stop to be chosen by our participants somewhere near Burnhamthorpe and Dixie.
